REVIEW

Riley Costantino October 26th, 2025

Smokey and the Bandit will always remain a personal favorite. If you're looking for older humor, romance, and a comedy all in one then you're in the right place. From Sheriff Buford T. Justice's improvised lines and jokes, to Jerry Reed's catchy song "East Bound and Down" made for this movie. Smokey and the Bandit

The chemistry between all of the characters and the way their jokes come off is hilarious and what makes this movie so rewatchable, and it will never get old. This movie promotes a country style of living, but also friendship and loyalty to the job.

Trivia

Did you know?

  • A majority of Buford T. Justice's lines were improvised.
  • The movie used 3 Trans-Ams. Towards the end of filming, these cars could barely run.
  • Jackie Gleason would ask for a "hamburger" from his assistant, which meant he wanted vodka.
