REVIEW

Riley Costantino October 26th, 2025

Beverly Hills Cop is the perfect combination of action and comedy. It gives you no-nonsense police work, but also gives you comedic scenes, especially during action fighting. The movie remains a classic, and one of the most beloved cop movies out there!

This movie also has the well known song "Axel F". The song was heavily popularized by Crazy Frog with their radio remix of the song.

Trivia

  • Eddie Murphy, the actor for Axel Foley, passed on a chance to act in the 1984 Ghostbusters film to be apart of BHC.
  • The city hall in the movie is the real Beverly Hills City Hall building. They replaced dying plants with new plants to make it look better in film.
  • The actor who played Inspector Todd, Gilbert Hill, was a former Detroit Police Homicide Detective and President of the City Council.
