In 1977, Big Enos wanted to drink Coors while watching a truck show. However, it was illegal to sell Coors without a permit east of the Mississippi. Bo "Bandit" Darville agrees to make the delivery in 28 hours, while picking up a hitchhiker and being pursued by Sheriff Buford T. Justice, angry that the runaway bride won't marry his son.
